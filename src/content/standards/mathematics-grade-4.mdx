## Virginia Grade 4 Mathematics Standards

This resource provides supplementary information and links to official Virginia Department of Education Standards of Learning (SOL) documents and instructional resources for Grade 4 Mathematics.

### Strands Covered

- **Number & Number Sense**: Understanding place value, comparing and ordering whole numbers, fractions, and decimals
- **Computation & Estimation**: Multiplication and division facts, multi-digit operations, estimation strategies
- **Measurement & Geometry**: Measuring length, weight, time, and temperature; identifying and describing geometric figures
- **Probability & Statistics**: Collecting, organizing, and interpreting data

### Key Focus Areas

Grade 4 students develop:
- Fluency with multiplication and division facts through 12 × 12
- Understanding of multi-digit multiplication and division 
- Fractional concepts including equivalence and comparison
- Introduction to decimal notation
- Geometric reasoning and measurement skills

### Additional Resources

For the most current standards and additional teaching resources, always refer to the official Virginia Department of Education website.
