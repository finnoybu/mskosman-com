## Warm-up (3 minutes)

Begin with a brief discussion to activate prior knowledge:
- Ask students what they already know about [topic]
- Share one interesting fact or question related to today's topic
- Preview the lesson objective

## Direct Instruction (5 minutes)

Present the core concept or skill:
- Explain key vocabulary and concepts
- Demonstrate the process or skill step-by-step
- Use visual aids or examples to support understanding
- Check for understanding with quick questions

## Guided Practice (4 minutes)

Students practice with teacher support:
- Work through 2-3 examples together as a class
- Students attempt problems/tasks with guidance
- Provide immediate feedback and clarification
- Monitor student work and address misconceptions

## Independent Practice (2 minutes)

Students apply learning independently:
- Students complete 1-2 practice problems/tasks on their own
- Circulate to observe and assist as needed
- Collect or review work to assess understanding

## Closure (1 minute)

Wrap up and reinforce learning:
- Review the lesson objective
- Ask students to summarize what they learned
- Preview the next lesson or homework if applicable
